    My name is Ben Cunningham and I'm an avid photographer, designer, and creative based in Boulder, CO. Growing up in California, I have always had a huge influence from the ocean and nature. This heavily inspired my photographic style and allowed me to work on countless projects in these settings growing up. This continued into college where I futhered this passion for nature and the outdoors.

    I have spent countless hours discovering new places, meeting new people, and capturing these moments. This has allowed me to hone my craft in many different subject matters of photography. Please reach out if you have any projects in mind that I can collaborate on. Whether it's portraits, skiing, skating, or anything in between, these photos allowed me to display the raw emotions and feelings of people and places.
